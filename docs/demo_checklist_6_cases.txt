PhishGuard Demo Checklist (6 Cases)

1) True Positive - Brand Spoof + VT Hit
- Example: faceb00k.com / lnstagram.com
- Expected: HIGH
- Show: brand_spoof_score high, vt_score high (if available)

2) True Positive - Suspicious Credential Link
- Example: temporary-url style credential lure
- Expected: HIGH
- Show: url_score near/high threshold and final HIGH

3) True Positive - Multi-link Phishing Body
- Example: body with suspicious link bundle
- Expected: HIGH
- Show: combined scoring (content + url + vt/brand)

4) True Negative - Legit College Form Email
- Example: forms.office.com from institute
- Expected: SAFE
- Show: low risk components and SAFE output

5) True Negative - Newsletter/Tracking Email
- Example: Twitch/Kotak with many links
- Expected: SAFE
- Show: reduced link_score, trusted-domain handling, faster response

6) True Negative - Plain Internal Text Email
- Example: no links, normal message
- Expected: SAFE
- Show: low total score and no hard-rule trigger

Quick talking points:
- Hybrid model: local ML + heuristics + VirusTotal evidence
- Speed optimization: VT top-3 risky links only
- Explainability: per-component debug and VT evidence summary in logs
