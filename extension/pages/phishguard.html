PhishGuard Education

Understand phishing risks and how PhishGuard protects you.

How PhishGuard analyzes emails

  • Checks suspicious links and domain reputation.
  • Detects brand spoof patterns and risky language.
  • Analyzes attachment names/types and file-hash reputation.
  • Combines all evidence into a final risk level.

Risk levels

HIGHStrong phishing evidence. Avoid clicking links or downloading attachments.

MEDIUMSome suspicious signals. Verify sender and purpose before acting.

SAFENo strong malicious indicators found in current analysis.

Safe habits

  • Never share passwords, OTPs, or bank details via email links.
  • Open suspicious files only after verifying with the sender.
  • Hover links and inspect domain spelling carefully.
  • Use "Report Phishing" when unsure.

PhishGuard is an assistive security tool and should be used with human judgment.
